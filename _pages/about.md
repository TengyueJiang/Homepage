Hi! I am Tengyue Jiang (Chinese name: 姜腾跃), a undergraduate student at ECUST, RA of Scalelab@SJTU, supervised by Prof. Yao Mu. My research focuses on Embodied AI, including Robotic Foundation Model.

## News
- 2025.07: I am currently an RA at MiLab@WLU supervised by Prof. Donglin Wang.
- 2025.09: I am currently an RA at Scalelab@SJTU supervised by Prof. Yao Mu.
- 2026.03: We released [MMaDA-VLA: Large Diffusion Vision-Language-Action Model with Unified Multi-Modal Instruction and Generation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25406)!
- 2026.06: We released [SA-VLA: State-aware tokenizer for improving Vision-Language-Action Models' performance](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.30113)!
- 2026.07: [MMaDA-VLA: Large Diffusion Vision-Language-Action Model with Unified Multi-Modal Instruction and Generation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25406) is accepted to ACM MM 2026!
